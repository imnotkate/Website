Who Are We?

Hailing (mostly) from Falkirk, Scotland - this four-piece regularly plays in both Glasgow and Edinburgh music scenes, making a name for themselves with their ability to adapt different indie styles into one coherent, catchy and unique sound. With raw lyrics on topics like romance, class and ageing written by Aiden Lyons (Rhythm Guitar and Vocals) hidden with layers of slacker pop, jazz, indie rock and a wee bit of punk - executed by Ryan Farmer (Bass), Craig Bachen (Drums) complete with the addition of Kate Rankin (Lead Guitar and resident Weegie).
